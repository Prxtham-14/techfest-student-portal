Innovate • Create • Inspire

Welcome to TechFest 2026, the biggest technology and innovation festival of Sunridge College of Engineering. Join students, innovators, programmers, designers, and robotics enthusiasts for an exciting journey of learning, competition, and creativity.

About Techfest 2026

TechFest 2026 is the annual technical festival of Sunridge College of Engineering. The festival brings together talented students from various disciplines to showcase their skills, exchange ideas, and compete in exciting technology-based events. The event includes coding competitions, robotics challenges, gaming tournaments, technical workshops, project exhibitions, and expert sessions from industry professionals. TechFest aims to inspire innovation, teamwork, and problem-solving among students while providing opportunities for networking and learning.

Why join Techfest?

  • Win Exciting Cash Prizes
  • Participate in National-Level Competitions
  • Learn from Industry Experts
  • Build Your Professional Network
  • Earn Certificates of Participation
  • Showcase Your Technical Skills
